What are Status Pages?

Status pages are public (or private) dashboards that show the health of your services. Keep your users informed about outages, maintenance windows, and system performance.

Real-time Updates Automatically reflects the current state of your checks
Access Control Public, password-protected, token-based, or domain-restricted
Embeddable Embed status widgets in your own website
Incident History Show past incidents with configurable retention

Creating a Status Page

Status pages are created through the Quismon dashboard. Each status page gets a unique URL:

https://status.quismon.com/your-slug

You can customize the appearance, select which checks to display, and configure access controls.
